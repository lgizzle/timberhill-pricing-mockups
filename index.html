Available Discounts

Corporate and insurance discounts are available for our joining fees. We also offer discounts for members age 65 and older and members of the military.

Contact one of our membership service representatives with any questions you may have or stop by to tour our facility.

Automatic Monthly Billing

The monthly dues rate is based on electronic funds transfer from your credit or debit card which we transmit on the 15th of each month.

Billing & Membership Policies

Late Fees

Payment for monthly membership dues must be made by the 15th of each month. A late fee of $10 will be added to your account for past due balances.

Consider auto-pay on your member account to avoid any potential late fees.

Changes to Membership

Any changes to your membership, including inactive status and addition or subtraction of members on your account, will take effect the following month. Additionally, if you choose have your membership transition to inactive, please note there is a one-month minimum requirement.

*Please note that all membership types are non-refundable
