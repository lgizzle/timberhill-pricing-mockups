Billing & Membership Policies

Monthly dues are billed via electronic funds transfer from your credit or debit card on the 15th of each month. A $10 late fee applies to past due balances. Consider auto-pay to avoid late fees.

Changes to Membership

Any changes, including inactive status or adding/removing members, take effect the following month. One-month minimum for inactive status. All memberships are non-refundable.
